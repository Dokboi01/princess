It's funny how a simple introduction by Kunle back in our 100lvl days could lead to the most beautiful chapter of my life. We started as friends, sharing laughs and secrets, until one day we stepped into something deeper (and yes, you asked me out first, no matter how many times you deny it! 😉).

We've been dating for almost 4 years now, and even though we've never had a "proper" date (I promise to make up for this very soon, my love!), our endless video calls are the absolute highlight of my days.

Watching the deep love they shared, I promised myself that when I found the one I wanted to spend forever with, I would call her that too. You are my Princess, today and always. 👑✨
